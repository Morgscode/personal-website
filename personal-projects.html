Find my next favourite song

I was pretty intrigued by digging through the documentation for the spotify api.

This is a fun little side-project that you can use to find new music on Spotify based on your favourite top songs, personal taste and mood. You can add songs you find to your 'liked songs' playlist on Spotify.

Technologies used: Spotify api, Vue.js, VueX, CSS Grid, Netlify
